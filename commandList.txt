**__MUSIC__**
play - link a youtube video and have it play
skip - skips the song currently playing
queue - lets you view the queue of songs
pause - pauses the player
resume - resumes the player
stop - clears the queue and leaves the voice channel


**__FUN__**
ping - pings the specified user (ex. ping wontonbot)
endconvo - the bot will end a conversation (ex. endconvo wontonbot)
startconvo - the bot will start a conversation (ex. startconvo wontonbot)
fish - blub x5
github - sends a link to the documentation of the bot
